about me

Hello, I'm Kateryna

My name is Kateryna Yarova and I am Web Designer. I aspire to create dynamic and interesting pages that delight people. I like plain and simple sites, but I am sad when I see a page on the Internet that doesn't attract you to visit this page again.


I create a web-site from scratch - from the formation of the site structure, creating sketches and thinking through the ways in which users will work with the site and then create colorful pictures and turning it into an interactive and live page. I also create pictures and banners, but my passion is CSS animation.


The days are gone when the old browsers were a burden and forced to abandon spectacular animations just because one browser creators are doing their job poorly. I believe that pleasing to the eye animation can greatly enhance the experience and make the site work more joyful.

I am sure that many more sites are waiting for me to become more attractive. Thank you for your attention, if you have any suggestions - I will respond to them with great pleasure.
