iFloor

iFloor is an interactive image plane to floor and walls. A projected image responds to movements of people or objects within projection area creating an immensely realistic illusion of various phenomena. For example, water movement and fish swimming away from under feet. Effects variability is limitless.
The effects can also represent different interactive games, football, for example, or speed leaves sweeping to score points.
iFloor is an impactful marketing tool. No potential client will pass by without paying attention to this almost fantastic visualization. An advertising message properly embedded into a special effect is perceived as an exciting amusement.

iSlingshot

SMS-Slingshot provides a possibility of shooting from a slingshot with SMS messages and their further appearance on building walls or billboards. Game participants enter messages via a screen of the multimedia slingshot, aim with an in-built laser and shoot as from a real slingshot.
An entered message appears on a wall where passers-by can view it. Powerful projectors are used to display messages. Messages form and background can represent your company logo. Content can also be displayed as a branded team game with several slingshots. Any creative idea can be implemented with SMS-Slingshot.

AR apps

Augmented reality allows completing a video stream from any camera with various objects. These objects appear when people or items move within a certain area creating a fully realistic view. It’s possible to add any character/animal/building/vehicle animation to the video, add weather or other effects.
The list of possible object is limited only by imagination. Objects are represented as 3D animation, video or images set played in succession. It’s possible to use an accompanying audio in addition to video.

Photo Wall

Photo Wall is a touch LCD panel where visitors can find their photos taken during an event. Pictures are passed to the wall in real time. It’s possible to apply effects to images and send them to an e-mail address or to a social network (with an event mark).

Kinect apps

Game applications and application for installations based on Microsoft Kinect1 and Kinect2. An interactive system based on a Kinect control unit allows creating applications and games where manipulating occurs with body movements and gestures. Kinect optics recognizes human skeleton without surface contact.
Kinect systems can be connected to computers which allows using various ways of displaying information: LCD panels, projectors, LED screens.

Oculus Rift apps

Oculus Rift is a new generation of virtual reality. Unlike TV screen or movie 3D participation effect is achieved with parallel displaying for each of the eyes. Your eyes perceive visual display in real place and time!
Your game vision is not stuck to a monitor anymore, your eyes can see everything happening around you.

Google card board

This is a one time augmented reality helmet based on an Android smartphone. It possesses all the Oculus Rift features, but doesn’t required computer connection.
Google card board allows you to examine pre-prepared three-dimensional spherical scenes in which the change of direction of view is achieved by turning the head.

Augmented Reality

Projects, which are implemented with Augmented Reality, are the main trend of modern marketing, including social media marketing. Such projects offer vast possibilities of integration with mobile devices and with social works. The main feature of AR-technology is a unique effect of involvement of brand target audience.
One more important advantage of AR-projects is opportunity to use classical methods of message transmission. Due to technological innovation of Augmented Reality, projects, based on it, are accompanied by the so-called «WOW-effect», which is able to surprise and motivate people of all ages, professions and cultures.
In this way, augmented reality is increasingly working for brands, and commercial and government organizations across the globe.
